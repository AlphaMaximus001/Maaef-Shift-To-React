Brand Strategy is the blueprint that shapes how people think, feel, and remember your business. Brand Strategy is a step-by-step long term actionable plans made by the businesses so that they can be recognized in this crowded world, basically by having their own unique identities such as their logo, website and social media handles but brand strategy does not only constitute of these identities and other factors also play a key role in making it unique. A unique brand identity is important in today’s world because it helps the SMEs for competing in the same market and within the same customer base every day hassle free by applying creative techniques to stand apart from its competitors. 
Whether you're a manufacturing company in Gujarat, a retail business in Delhi, a technology startup in Bengaluru, or a local service provider in Lucknow, your brand can become your biggest competitive advantage. 
Brand Strategy goes far beyond designing a logo or choosing brand colours. It answers important questions such as-
Why does your business exist? 
What problem do you solve? 
Who is your ideal customer? 
Why should customers choose you instead of competitors?
What emotions should people associate with your brand?
Why Brand Strategy Matters for Indian SMEs?
Many SMEs believe branding is only for multinational corporations or large enterprises. In reality, branding is often even more important for smaller businesses because they need to compete with larger players using limited resources.
A strong brand strategy helps SMEs by-
1- Building customer trust and credibility
2- Differentiate from local and national competitors
3- Increase customer loyalty
4- Reduce dependence on price discounts
5- Improve marketing efficiency
6- Attract better talent and business partnerships
7- Create long-term business value
Customers are more likely to buy from businesses they recognize, trust, and remember.
The Core Elements of a Successful Brand Strategy:
1- Brand Purpose:
Brand purpose is what gives your business direction and meaning.
Example- A furniture company is not only in the business of making furniture, it is also enabling creation of sustainable living of Indian people.
2- Target Audience:
Knowing and understanding your customers is the key to getting your branding right. You should include Age, Income, Geography, Lifestyle, Occupational, Pain points, and Buying behaviour. The better you know your customers, the better your brand would perform.
3- Brand Positioning:
Brand Positioning refers to a distinct and unique position that brand occupies in the consumers mind. These are the questions which needs to be asked- 
What makes us stand out?
What value do we provide?
Why should consumers trust us?
A clear positioning statement makes your brand memorable.
4- Brand Personality:
Imagining your brand as a person, what type of personality would it have?
Would it be: 
Professional, Friendly, Innovative, Reliable, Premium, Traditional, Young.
The personality of the brand must be the same for every customer interaction.
5- Brand Messaging:
Messaging refers to: 
Tagline, Value proposition, Elevator pitch, Copy, Social media, Sales pitches.
Having consistent messaging builds brand recognition.
6- Visual Identity:
Visual brand can include: 
Logo, Color scheme, Font, Packaging, Style of photography, Marketing genre.
Good design increases recognition but must still fit to the greater strategy of the brand.
7- Customer Experience:
The interaction of customers helps brand-building process.
Customer touch points may include the following: 
Website, Customer service, Product quality, Delivery, After sales service, Social media
Great brands are great because they give the perfect customer experience. 
The Future of Branding for Indian SMES:
The digital economy in India is expanding. Nowadays most buyers conduct research on a brand before making a purchase. The website of the brand must look professional, the social media presence must be authentic, and the reviews must be positive. Keeping all of this in mind, it can surely be stated that those SMEs which will invest in branding strategies today will be among the leaders in the future.
